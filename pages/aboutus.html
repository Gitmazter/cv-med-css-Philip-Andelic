A Bit About Me

I moved to Vietnam in 2018 for a change of pace and a job as an English teacher.

I spent the following 4 years teaching English and dealing with the pandemic lockdown. During this time I learned that I loved to code. The natural course of action was to learn more about it so I applied for an Online Blockchain Development Degree from Medieinstitutet, Sweden.

What started as a hobby grew into a fully fledged passion that motivated me to do better every day. To be faster and more accurate, to make websites more pretty, to write scripts that work smoother.

After some thinking I applied to a online course at the Swedish Media Institute and I haven't looked back since. I am still a student but I'm working hard to reach my goal. You can follow my progress here, I promise to update regularly.
